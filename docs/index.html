What is Geometry Dash?

Geometry Dash is a single button skill game.

Runs on:

  • Mobile
  • Desktop
  • Some consoles

Gamemodes

  1. Cube
  2. Ship
  3. Ball
  4. UFO
  5. Wave
  6. Robot
  7. Spider

Controls

Single button press, tapping or holding the button.
In platformer mode, you can also use the arrow keys to move left and right.
However, on mobile they added arrow buttons on the sides of the screen.

Gamemode Click / Hold
Cube Click to jump, hold to keep jumping.
Ship Click to go up, release to go down.
Ball Click to flip gravity.
UFO Click to jump even in mid-air.
Wave Click to change direction up 45 degrees release to change back.
Robot Click to jump, hold for a higher jump.
Spider Click to change gravity and teleport to the opposite side.
